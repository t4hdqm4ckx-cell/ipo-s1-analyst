# Report Template

When you call `complete_analysis`, your findings will be assembled into a Markdown report using this structure. Fill every field with the depth and precision of a senior IB analyst memo.

---

```
# IPO S-1 Analysis: {company_name}
**Filed:** {filing_date} | **Analyst:** S-1 Analyst Agent | **Date:** {today}
**Recommendation:** {BUY | HOLD | PASS} — {one-line rationale}

---

## Executive Summary
{2–4 paragraph summary covering: what the company does, its financial profile,
the IPO thesis, and the bottom-line recommendation with key supporting reasons}

---

## 1. Business Overview
{Products, services, markets, revenue model, TAM/SAM if disclosed}

## 2. Products & Services
{Detailed breakdown of each product/service line, pricing, unit economics if available}

## 3. Financial Analysis
{Revenue (with YoY growth), gross margin, EBITDA/operating margin,
net income/loss, burn rate, cash & equivalents, debt, cash runway.
Use actual figures from the filing.}

### Key Financial Metrics
| Metric | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
...

## 4. Valuation
{Implied IPO valuation, EV/Revenue multiple, EV/EBITDA, comparison to sector comps.
Fair value assessment.}

### Comparable Companies
| Company | EV/Rev | EV/EBITDA | Rev Growth | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
...

## 5. Competitive Landscape
{Named competitors, market share estimates, differentiation, moat analysis}

## 6. SWOT Analysis
### Strengths
- {specific, quantified where possible}

### Weaknesses
- {honest, material}

### Opportunities
- {market expansion, product, geographic}

### Threats
- {competitive, regulatory, macro, technology}

## 7. Funding History & Key Backers
{All disclosed funding rounds with dates, amounts, and lead investors.
Total capital raised pre-IPO. Notable VC/PE backers.}

## 8. Use of Proceeds
{How IPO proceeds will be deployed. Growth vs. insider liquidity split.}

## 9. Cap Table & Dilution
{Pre/post-IPO ownership by major stakeholders. Dilution to new investors.
Lock-up provisions.}

## 10. Risk Flags
{Top 10 material risks, not boilerplate. Include going-concern if applicable.}

## 11. Management Assessment
{Key executives, relevant experience, red flags in compensation or related-party transactions}

{What has to go right for strong outperformance}

- {risk 1}
```
